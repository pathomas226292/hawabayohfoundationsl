What Are the Signs of Autism?

Recognizing the early signs of autism can make a world of difference. Common indicators include limited eye contact, delayed speech, repetitive behaviors, and difficulty with social interaction. Every child is different, and symptoms can vary widely in severity. Early diagnosis through observation and professional screening allows for timely intervention, which greatly improves developmental outcomes

How Can Autistic Children Be Inclusive in Society?

Inclusion begins with awareness and acceptance. Schools and communities can foster inclusivity by promoting autism education, providing support aids, and encouraging peer understanding. Simple adjustments—like quiet spaces, visual cues, or flexible communication—can empower autistic children to participate confidently in everyday activities. When we embrace neurodiversity, society becomes richer, more compassionate, and truly inclusive.
